HI! I AM SAUMIA.

I am a third year undergraduate at IIT Roorkee, majoring in Mechanical Engineering. I am a Machine Learning and interaction design enthusiast.

I recently worked with Dr. Caleb Ronald at Indian Institue of Sciences(IISc), Bangalore on the topic "A Microscopic Traffic Simulator Integrated Driving Simulator". In this project, I developed a server using Sockets for the real-time exchange of data between a traffic simulator, VISSIM and a gaming engine, Unity3D. This whole project was furthur transferred to Oculus Rift, for better user-experience. We are furthur planning to transfer this project to big screens while taking user-input from controllers.

I am also a member of the design and development section of campus group Design Studio. As a part of this group, I worked on various projects related to App-development and Augmented Reality. I also worked on various design projects, which involved designing posters, flexes for Cognizance, the annual tech-event of IITR.
