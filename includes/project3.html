PLAN-ET

Design Studio, IITR

Date

Feb 1, 2017 to March 18, 2017

Category

Augmented Reality, Game development

Contributers

Saumia Singhal
Rachit Raj
Anant Agarwal

Plan-et is an Augmented Reality based treasure hunting game. It entertains and challenges users to find the winning prize hidden inside the campus, by solving time-bound hints. In order to unlock the next hint, the player has to scan the correct place inside IIT-R campus, using his/her mobile phone.

This game was played during Srishti, the annual cultural fest of IIT-R.
