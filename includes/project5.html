APARNA WOMEN SOCIALIZER

Women Hackathon, 2018

Date

March 10, 2018 to March 20, 2018

Category

Android Development

Contributers

Saumia Singhal

It is an app developed focussing on the idea of Women Empowerment in remote areas. This app involves the basic features of various other social media apps like facebook, instagram etc. It also works as an free-lancing platform, where women can upload their small projects, and get in touch with others, working in the same field, or who are trying to learn that skill.
